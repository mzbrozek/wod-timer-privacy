Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

WOD Timer does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your health and workout data stays on your device.

What We Collect

WOD Timer does not collect any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device and does not transmit any data to external servers.

Apple HealthKit

WOD Timer uses Apple HealthKit solely to keep the Apple Watch timer running when the screen dims during a workout (background execution). Specifically:

  • A workout session is started when you begin a timer on Apple Watch.
  • The session is ended automatically when the timer completes or is reset.
  • No health data is read, written, stored, or transmitted by WOD Timer.
  • HealthKit data is never shared with third parties, used for advertising, or combined with other data.

Data Storage

WOD Timer stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your timer configuration preferences (workout type, duration, rounds).
  • App settings (haptic style, sound preference).

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

WOD Timer does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or tracking frameworks. There are no third-party services that receive your data.

Children's Privacy

WOD Timer does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no data collection of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
